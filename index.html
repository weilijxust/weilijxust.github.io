Brief CV

Currently I am an associate professor of the School of Information Engineering in Jiangxi University of Science and Technology. I received my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Jiangxi University of Science and Technology and South China Agricultural University in June 2008 and June 2018, respectively. From 2016 to 2017, I was a visiting professor at the University of Calgary in Canada. And I am the steering committee chair of the annual Springer International Symposium on Intelligence Computation and Applications (ISICA) since 2015. I published over 30+ research papers in the international conferences and journals and have served as program and local arrangement chairs for 10+ international conferences. .

Research Interests

My current research interests mainly include Deep Learning, Machine Learning and Computational Intelligence Algorithm. More specifically, I am interested in: Particle Swarm Optimization, Differential Evolution and Ant Colony Optimization, Multi/Many Objective Optimization, Fitness Landscape Analysis and their applications in practical optimization problems.
